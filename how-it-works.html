Science-based approach:

Losing weight and keeping it off doesn’t happen by starving yourself, going on a detox or using your gym membership once a month. Decades of research in medicine, nutrition and psychology show that long-term weight loss is the result of gradual changes to the bad habits that got us overweight in the first place. SmartHabits guides you on this journey, helping you to gradually adjust your nutrition, exercise routine and motivation every day so that you lose weight and keep it off.

Your personal team of
health professionals:

A dedicated team of health professionals work with you to optimise your nutrition, fitness, and motivation and mindset. Your team - including a Dietician, Psychologist and Personal Trainer – tailor their approach to get the best out of you. Along the way they’ll teach you the science behind weight loss, nutrition and fitness, while helping you create smart exercise and eating habits that set you up for long-term weight loss and health

Smart Technology
tracks your weight, activity and
sleep seamlessly.

People who lose weight and keep it off have one thing in common: they set weight and fitness goals, and track their progress against these goals. You manage what you measure, and this is particularly true when it comes to weight loss and exercise. SmartHabits provides you with a wireless scale and activity tracker, and integrates with some of the best smart phone health apps, so you can track your weight and exercise with no hassles.

Interactive Online Data Bank:

Your smart devices and apps send a stream of information to your online data bank, allowing you to explore your progress in a number of engaging ways. Your team of health professionals use your data to identify what’s working well for you, to see how you’re progressing and, if you are slipping off track, to help you get back on course.

Convenient online video
coaching sessions:

Coaching sessions with your team take place over Skype, a popular video conferencing service available on a range of internet-enabled devices. Your team is available from 6 am to 8pm six days a week which means you can schedule your sessions at a time and place that best suits you. Your online sessions involve a one-on-one session with your Nutritionist, Psychologist or Personal Trainer and focus on building the skills, knowledge and behaviours that you require to build healthy, smart habits. Rest assured, research shows there is no difference in weight loss outcomes for video-based coaching compared to face-to-face sessions.
